Introduction

This page presents a curated list of underexplored refactorings identified in our work An Empirical Study of Software Refactorings in Real-world Open-Source Java Projects. These refactorings were uncovered through empirical analysis and mining of open-source repositories, revealing opportunities for further research and improved tool support.

Categories of Underexplored Refactorings
